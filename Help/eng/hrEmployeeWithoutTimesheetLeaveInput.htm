Employees not Subject to Time Card Control

Entry screen

Field descriptions

Date from/to

The period in which workday of the employee can be calculated though there is no timekeeping record.

Shift / Workday

These are required fields on which the system will base to calculate the workdays for the employee.

Hours

Number of working hours of the employee; required field.
In case there are non-working days during the period (Date from/to), this number will be subtracted by the one defined in “Non-working Days/ Public Holidays”.

For example:
- Hours in this function is 8;
- Non-working days include 4 hours on Saturday and 8 hours on Sunday;
- The working hours of employee will be calculated as 4 hours on Saturday and 0 hour on Sunday.

Note:
In case there are overtime working hours, it is required to define in “Employee Workday Additional”.
