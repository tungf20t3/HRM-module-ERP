FA Depreciation Allocation Entry

You can create FA depreciation allocation entry to post to the general ledger.

Entry screen

Operations

Create

Press F4 or click - “Create” toolbar icon to create allocation entries in general ledger

Delete

Press F8 or click - “Delete” toolbar icon to delete created allocation entries. They can be only deleted in this function, not in Journal Voucher (JV).

Print

Press F6 or click - “Print” toolbar icon to print created allocation entries
