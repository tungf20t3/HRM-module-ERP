Leave Time Detailed Report

Filter Condition

Descriptions

This report is used to show list of employees having days of leave (workday category as other) in a period of date from/to.
Days and hours of leave are system-generated by the workday sign and hours defined in:

    - Non-working Days, Public Holidays;
    - Employee Leave Input;
    - No. of Working Hours per Day (optional parameter);
    - If there is information in Employee Card Info Input, days and hours of leave will be system-generated by the workday defined in this function.

For example:
- Non-working days, public holidays: 01 Jan 2013, workday sign is A1 and number of working hours is 9;
- Employee leave input: from 02 Jan 2013 to 02 Jan 2013, workday sign is A2 and number of working hours is 12;
- Optional parameter "No. of working hours per day" is 6.
After workday computation, the "Leave Time Detailed Report" from 01 Jan 2013 to 02 Jan 2013 will be shown as:
+ On 01 Jan 2013: workday A1, 1.5 days and 9 hours;
+ On 02 Jan 2013: workday A2, 2 days and 12 hours.
- Employee card info input: from 01 Jan 2013 to 01 Jan 2013, workday sign is A3 and number of working hours is 6.
After workday computation, the "Leave Time Detailed Report" from 01 Jan 2013 to 02 Jan 2013 will be shown as:
+ On 01 Jan 2013: workday A1, 1 day and 6 hours;
+ On 02 Jan 2013: workday A2, 2 days and 12 hours.
