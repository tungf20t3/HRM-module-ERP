Special Journal Setup

This function is used to define parameters of special journals in general journal books.

    Sales
    Purchase
    Cash Receipt
    Cash Disbursement

'td2"> Purchase Journal

Report Form (Circular No.15/2006/QĐ-BTC)

Descriptions

Credit Account/Title 1, 2

Credit account data are shown in column 1, account name and English name in titles.

Debit Account 1/Title 1, 2

Debit account data are shown in column 2, account name and English name in titles.

Debit Account 2/Title 1, 2

Debit account data are shown in column 3, account name and English name in titles.

Debit Accounts Excluded

List of accounts of which arising is not recorded in purchase journal (but general journal)
