List of PIT on Salaries or Wages by Partially Progressive Tariff (Form 05-1/BK-QTT-TNCN)

Report Layout

Descriptions of data generating

- Column [11]:

Total assessable income and adjusted income;

- Column [12]:

Tax-reducible income of individuals with labor contracts in a term of over 3 months (working in the Economic Zone);

- Column [13]:

Tax-reducible income of individuals with labor contracts in a term of over 3 months (by Agreements);

- Column [19]:

Taxable income minus (-) deductions (taxpayer’s family circumstances + Charitable, Humanitarian or Study Promotional + Compulsory Insurances + Voluntary Pension);

- Column [20]:

Deducted PIT of resident individuals with labor contracts in a term of over 3 months;

- Column [21]:

Reduced PIT of individuals with labor contracts in a term of 3 months or over (working in the Economic Zone);

- Column [22]:

Total Payable PIT of individuals with authorized PIT finalization;

- Column [23]:

Overpaid PIT equals deducted PIT minus payable PIT (of individuals with authorized PIT finalization). The result will be shown if it is a positive number; otherwise, it will be shown as 0.

- Column [24]:

Payable PIT of individuals with authorized PIT finalization

Note:
To view this declaration, it’s required to run PIT calculation and PIT finalization process.
