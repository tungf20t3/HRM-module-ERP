Stock Coverage Report

Filter condition

This report shows the stock quantity and value of items in Receipts with date before or the same as one in "Date to" field backward a period of time based on the "Analysis Period" and "Analysis Time".

For example:
Item VTA, on 15 Feb 2013, received 2,000 with unit price of VND 100,000;
On 15 Mar 2013, received 3,000 with unit price of VND 100,000;
On 25 Mar 2013, issued 500 with unit price of VND 100,000;
On 15 Apr 2013, issued 300 with unit price of VND 100,000.

- View report to date 31 Mar 2013, analysis period as 2 - Month and analysis time as 1 month.
To 31 Mar 2013, the stock quantity of the Receipt dated 15 Feb 2013 is 1,500 (equals quantity of the Receipt dated 15 Feb 2013 minus quantity of the Issue dated 25 Mar 2013), the stock quantity of the Receipt dated 15 Mar 2013 is still 3,000.
Therefore, the stock quantity of item VTA shown in report is 1,500 (equals stock quantity of the Receipt dated 15 Feb 2013 because it is before 31 Mar 2013 backward 1 month; quantity in the Receipt dated 15 Mar 2013 is not added).
The stock value of this item will be 2,000 * 100,000 – 500 * 100,000 = 150,000,000 VND.

- View report to date 30 Apr 2013, analysis period as 2 - Month and analysis time as 2 month.
To 30 Apr 2013, the stock quantity of the Receipt dated 15 Feb 2013 is 1,200 (equals quantity of the Receipt dated 15 Feb 2013 minus quantity of the Issue dated 25 Mar 2013 and Issued dated 15 Apr 2013), the stock quantity of the Receipt dated 15 Mar 2013 is still 3,000.
Therefore, the stock quantity of item VTA shown in report is 1,200 (equals stock quantity on 30 Apr 2013 of the Receipt dated 15 Feb 2013 because it is before 30 Apr 2013 backward 2 month; quantity in the Receipt dated 15 Mar 2013 is not added).
The stock value of this item will be 2,000 * 100,000 – 500 * 100,000 - 300 * 100,000 = 120,000,000 VND.

- View report to date 30 Apr 2013, analysis period as 1 - Day and analysis time as 30 days.
To 30 Apr 2013, the stock quantity of the Receipt dated 15 Feb 2013 is 1,200 (equals quantity of the Receipt dated 15 Feb 2013 minus quantity of the Issue dated 25 Mar 2013 and Issued dated 15 Apr 2013), the stock quantity of the Receipt dated 15 Mar 2013 is still 3,000.
Therefore, the stock quantity of item VTA shown in report is 4,200 (equals stock quantity of the Receipt dated 15 Feb 2013 plus one in the Receipt dated 15 Mar 2013 because they are before 30 Apr 2013 backward 30 days).
The stock value of this item will be 2,000 * 100,000 – 500 * 100,000 - 300 * 100,000 + 3,000 * 100,000 = 420,000,000 VND.
