Cash Receipt Allocation to Invoices

“Cash Receipt Allocation to Invoices” function is used to allocate amount of customer entered in Receipt or Credit Advice in “Cash Management” with Transaction Type “2 –Receipt from customer” and “5 – FC Receipt with Average Book Rate” to invoices issued to that customer.

“Cash Receipt Allocation to Invoices” Screen

Receipt or Credit Advice in “Cash Management” will be listed in this screen with Transaction type “2 – Receipt from Customer” and “5 – FC Receipt with Average Book Rate”. Besides, “Debit/Credit Memo” vouchers are also shown in this screen to allocate to invoices.

Allocation Screen

This screen lists the invoices unsettled by customer in chosen Receipt.

Inv. No

Number of invoices unpaid or partially paid by customer, extracted from list of invoices to be settled.

Inv. Date

The date the invoice is created.

FC Code

Code of transaction currency in invoice

Cr. Acct.

Credit account in decrease receivable from customer transaction. It is automatically updated based on receivable account used to reflect transaction. Cr. Account in this case cannot be entered but extracted from invoice.

Invoice Amt.

Amount in transaction currency in invoice; automatically updated.

Paid

Amount paid to invoice; automatically updated (converted to transaction currency in invoice). .

Payable

Amount receivable from customer (in transaction currency); automatically updated based on amount paid to invoice (in currency in invoice).

[FC] Receipt

Receipt in transaction currency of debit memo.

Converted Payment

If transaction currency in credit memo is different from that in invoice, Converted Payment field will be updated to have amount converted to transaction currency in invoice. Otherwise; this field will not be updated.

Description

Transaction reasons/ memo, posted to GL as Description field.

Receipt

Amount receivable in GL currency. If transaction currency is the same as GL currency, this field will not be displayed. This field value is equal to FC amount multiplied with exchange rate, but editable.
