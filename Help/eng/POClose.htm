PO Close

Common tasks in closing PO

Close PO

In PO browser, check on PO to close, then click on - “Close” to close PO.

Undo PO Close

In some cases, you are required to undo “Close” of a PO to operate some previous steps.
Click “ - “Search” to filter closed PO. All PO in “Close” status meeting filter condition are listed.
Check on PO to undo close
Click on - “Roll Back” to back status before closed.
