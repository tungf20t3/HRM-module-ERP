Cost Factor Group List

Path: Modules → Advanced Costing → List Maintenance → Cost Factor Group List


Cost factor group is used to group cost factors in reports viewing; not used in product costing.

For example: Production overheads (627) can be divided into many cost factors by aggregation and allocation formulas.

Cost factor 6271 is aggregated by direct department and allocated by material.
Cost factor 6272 is aggregated by indirect department and allocated by coefficient.
In costing reports, it is required to group cost factors 6271 & 6272 into a group for management purpose.
Cost factor group is created for production overhead; cost factors 6272 & 6272 are created in Cost Factor List with cost factor group selected as production overhead.
