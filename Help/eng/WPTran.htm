“Write-off”

This function is used to delete the pending amount in invoices which have actually not been collected or over paid by customers.
This amount reflects incorrectly receivable status.

Note:
It is unnecessary to run this function if you do not control receivables and receipt detailed by invoice. Before running this function, you must declare turnover write-off account and expense write-off account in optional parameters in General Ledger module.

Example: Supplier A’s statement on 01/02/2006:

Remaining amount which needs paying to Supplier A in payables list by invoice on 01/02/2006 is: 1 000 + 1 500 000=1 501 000.
Credit balance of Supplier A in detailed payables book with account 331 on 01/02/2006 is 1000 – 2 000 + 1 500 000=1 499 000

Closing Cr. Balance: 1 500 000
