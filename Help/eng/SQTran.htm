Quotation

Field descriptions - format view

Customer

Customer who requires quotation; extracted from Customer/Supplier Master, required field.

Contact Person

Representative of customer who requires quotation or makes requisition; free enter and optional.

Effective to

The effective period of quotation required by customer in requisition

Memo

Description of reason, purpose… to require quotation

Voucher No.

Number of Quotation; automatically numbered by the system as declaration in voucher list system or entered by user.

Voucher Date

The date when the Quotation is created.

Ex. Rate

Exchange rate; used in case of Quotation in foreign currency. Select FC code and enter exchange rate. Exchange rate is implicitly extracted from each type of FC in “Exchange Rate List”, in the latest date.

Status

There are 3 statuses of Quotation: “No Action”, “Open” and “Closed”.

Field descriptions - “Details” Tab

Item Code

Items in Quotation; extracted from Item Master, required field.

UOM

Default unit of measurement in Item Master but can be changed by conversion of units of measurement in some special cases.

Site

Other information of items site in quotation

Quantity

Quantity of items need quoting to customer; required field.

FC Base Price

Sales price defined in “Sales Price List” in foreign currency; un-editable

FC Quoted Price

Price of item in foreign currency sent to customer in particular conditions

FC Amount

Arising amount in foreign currency

FC Discount

Discount amount in foreign currency applied to customer who buys this item

Tax Code/ Tax Rate

Tax Code and Tax Rate of item; extracted from Item Master.

Due Date

Delivery date required by customer

Base Price

Sales price defined in “Sales Price List” in GL currency; un-editable

Quoted Price

Price of item in GL currency sent to customer in particular conditions. It will be equal to defined Sales Price if there is definition in “Sales Price List”. In case the item in quotation is discounted and this discount is not defined to “Apply in Extended Amount”, quoted price will be equal to sales price minus discount.

Amount

Arising amount in GL currency

Discount

Discount amount in quotation having discount which is defined to “Apply in Extended Amount”

Job

Code of job; used to control a sale process from this quotation. If next steps are extracted data from this quotation, job will be automatically enclosed.

Field descriptions - “Charges” Tab

“Charges” Tab in Quotation is declared as information for reference, used to declare predicted charges which can be arise. There is no interaction to other next steps now.

Charge Code

Charge code is attached to Quotation, extracted from Charge List. This field may not be entered if there is no arising.

Amount

Amount of charge related to sales if SO is carried out. Amount of each charge code will be allocated to each item in quotation by specific formula of each code. Allocation formula depends on Charge List creating.

Field descriptions - “Shipping Information” Tab

Ship-to

Place where goods are delivered to customer; extracted from Customer List – Address. This is an optional field.

Ship via

Means of shipping goods; extracted from Transport Type List. This is an optional field.

Working with attachments

The program allows users to add attachment files to a voucher. To add an attachment file, click on the icon then choose file. To download an attached file, click on the icon . To remove an attached file on a voucher, click on the icon .
Program allows attachments up to 10 MB, the maximum size of each file can be declared. (Refer this function to counsellors)
