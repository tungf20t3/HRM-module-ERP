Updated with latest financial, accounting and tax regulations

AI ERP is always updated following latest regulations of the Ministry of Finance.

Multi-currency data management

AI ERP allows multi-currency management. All transactions can be input and saved in transaction currency and can be exchanged to base currency.
You are also enabled to select the booked rate valuated by FIFO, monthly average, moving average, specific method or actual exchange rate. During calculation, the system will auto-generate booked rate and create exchange rate difference entry. At the end of period, the system will recalculate the booked rate of each account, customer/supplier and automatically re-create the exchange rate difference entries arising in period.
The system can exchange the payment amount into the currency of AR/AP management in case of payment in base currency. The reports can be printed in base currency or in both transaction and base currencies.

Multi-unit data management

AI ERP helps to manage data of multiple units, view reports of each unit or of the whole company (all units).
