Purchase Receipt – Direct Issue Voucher

In some neat management business like building by project, when purchasing, accounting department will issue directly to project without Issue vouchers.
About inventory data control, the system will automatically create an illusory issue voucher with item code and quantity the same as receipt. About issuing price, if you select “SP. Cost”, the system will assign receiving price as issuing price; otherwise, when calculating issuing price at the end of month (average, moving average, FIFO), the system will assign the price to PXG issue like other vouchers.
About accounting, “Purchase Receipt – Direct Issue Voucher” is the same as Purchase Invoice, with one more double entry showing the issuing transaction (Debit of direct issue expense/ Credit on inventory account).
Deleting duplicate entries when accounting cash purchase is the same as purchase invoice; it means the entries are not posted to general ledger, except the part issued for using.

Field descriptions – “Details” tab

The same as Purchase Receipt and some more fields in entry screen:

Direct Issue Expense Acct.

Extracted from Item List; used as debit account in Direct Issue Voucher.

“SP. Cost” option

Used for items cost defined by average, moving average and special cost methods
“SP. Cost” is default as “Marked” when creating new voucher. At that time, issue price and amount equal receipt price and amount.
