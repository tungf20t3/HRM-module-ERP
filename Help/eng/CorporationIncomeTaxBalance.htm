Corporation Income Tax Balance Sheet

Article definition screen


Field descriptions

Appendix Title

If selected “Yes”, this row will not belong to report content but title of appendix. (For example: “D. Besides the appendix of this declaration, we enclose following documents:")

Belong to Appendix

If selected “Yes”, this row will belong to appendix content.

Calculating Way

There are 2 options to select the way to calculate value of each article in report:

    - 0 - Base on Formula: there is 1 formula for each article, this formula includes addition and subtraction only.
    - 1 – Base on Acct. Arising: debit, credit accounts can be user-defined. According to user-defined options, the system will only get the arising amount in debit or credit arising, or in debit – credit corresponding. If arising amount is defined to get in debit – credit corresponding, the deduction option will be not included. If arising amount is defined to get in debit or credit, select 1 in 2 deduction options.

Non-deduction Accts.

If arising amount is defined to get in debit or credit only, select to define if the deduction is included or not.

For example:
Credit of account 511: 10.000.000
Debit of account 511 corresponding to credit of account 531: 1.000.000
Debit of account 511 corresponding to credit of account 911: 9.000.000
There are 2 ways to declare to get 9.000.000:
- Debit account is 511, credit account is 911
- Credit account is 511, account without deduction is 911
