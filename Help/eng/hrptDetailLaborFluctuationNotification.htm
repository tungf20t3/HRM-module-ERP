Detail of Labor Fluctuation Notification

Filter Condition

Special field descriptions

The labor fluctuation shown in this report by following parts:

Labor information at the beginning and ending of period

Beginning

Total of labor

Number of employees with the information satisfying the filter condition; “Start Date” of the “Official Position” in “Working History/ Current Working History” must be latest and before the “Date from” in the filter condition. In case there is no information in “Working History”, the system will count employees based on their “Join Date” in employee general information. The employees with “Date of Leaving” before “Date from” will not be shown in report.

Qualifications (column 4 – 9)

The information shown in these columns are based on “Time of Report” (column 1) and “Report Form Parameter Declaration”. “Education Level” (Regular Education) is “Major Certificate” and “Graduation Date” must be latest and before “Date from” in the filter condition. In case there is no record in “Regular Education” satisfying the filter condition, the system will get the “Education Level” from “General Information” with the “Graduation Date” before the “Date from”.

Type of Labor Contract (column 10 – 13)

Type of labor contract is based on “Contract Type” in “Labor Contract” input; only contracts with “Date Signed” before “Date from” will be shown in this report.

Total labor joining UI (column 14)

Number of employees joining unemployment insurance are counted by the “Time of Report” and definition in “Employee Insurance and Union Information”. Only records with the “Effective Date from” before the “Date from” and the “Effective Date to” after the “Date from” will be get to report.

Ending

Total of Labor

Number of employees with the information satisfying the filter condition; “Start Date” of the “Official Position” in “Working History/ Current Working History” must be latest and before or the same as the “Date to” in the filter condition”. In case there is no information in “Working History”, the system will count employees based on their “Join Date” in employee general information. The employees with “Date of Leaving” before or the same as “Date to” will not be shown in report.

Qualifications (column 4 – 9)

The information shown in these columns are based on “Time of Report” (column 1) and “Report Form Parameter Declaration”. “Education Level” (Regular Education) is “Major Certificate” and “Graduation Date” must be latest and before or the same as the “Date to” in the filter condition. In case there is no record in “Regular Education” satisfying the filter condition, the system will get the “Education Level” from “General Information” with the “Graduation Date” before the “Date to”.

Type of Labor Contract (column 10 – 13)

Type of labor contract is based on “Contract Type” in “Labor Contract” input; only contracts with “Date Signed” before or the same as “Date to” will be shown in this report.

Total labor joining UI (column 14)

Number of employees joining unemployment insurance are counted by the “Time of Report” and definition in “Employee Insurance and Union Information”. Only records with the “Effective Date from” before or the same as the “Date to” and the “Effective Date to” after the “Date to” will be get to report.

List of Labor Decreased

This part shows the employees who stop working (Date of Leaving) within the report period.

ID Card No.

Identity card number is get from employee general information or “Passport No.” in (Passport, Visa).

SI Book No.

Number of social insurance book is get from “Employee Insurance and Union Information”.

Qualifications (column 7a – 7e)

These columns will be checked (X) on the basic of definition in “Report Form Parameter Declaration”. “Education Level” (Regular Education) is “Major Certificate” and “Graduation Date” must be latest and before or the same as the “Date to” in the filter condition. In case there is no record in “Regular Education” satisfying the filter condition, the system will get the “Education Level” from “General Information” with the “Graduation Date” before the “Date to”.

Type of Labor Contract (column 8a – 8c)

These columns will be checked (X) on the basic of definition in “Report Form Parameter Declaration” and the “Contract Type” in “Labor Contract” input; only contracts with latest “Date Signed” before or the same as “Date to” will be used for this report.

The Effective Date of the Labor Contract (column 9)

“Effective Date from” in “Labor Contract”; only contracts with latest “Date Signed” before or the same as “Date to” will be used for this report.

The Terminative Date of the Labor Contract (column 10)

“Date of Leaving” in employee information input

Be Employee Joining UI (column 11)

This column will be checked (X) on the basic of definition in “Employee Insurance and Union Information Input”: the “Effective from” must be before or the same as the “Date to” and the “Effective to” must be after the “Date from” in filter condition.

Reason of Decrease (column 12 - 16)

These columns will be checked (X) on the basic of definition in “Report Form Parameter Declaration”.

List of Labor Increased

This part shows the employees who start working (Join Date) within the report period.

List of employees increased in period (column 1 – 8)

The data of these columns are the same as ones in Labor - Ending

The Effective Date of the Labor Contract (column 9)

“Effective Date from” in “Labor Contract”; only contracts with latest “Date Signed” before or the same as “Date to” will be used for this report.

Total of UI Joining Time (if any) (column 10)

The data in this column will be get from “UI Joining Time” in “Labor Fluctuation Input” with the “Fluctuation Date” within the period in filter condition.

Changing Labor’s Information

This part shows list of employees with changes about: salary, position, qualifications or form of contract commitment within the report period.
Changes definition:

Salary Change

In “Employee Information/ Salary History/ Current Actual Salary History, the “Official” records with “Start Date” within the report period will be used for report.

Note: the “Basic Salary” of the satisfied record will be the “New Salary” and one of the record right before that record will be the “Old Salary” in the report.

Position Change

In “Employee Information/ Current Working History, the records with “Start Date” within the report period will be used for report.

Note:
Only records with “Current” / “Official Position” and “Change Category” as 3 – Position Transfer will be used for report. The “Position” of the satisfied record will be the “New Position” and the “Official Position” of the record right before that record will be the “Old Position” in the report.

Education Level Change

In “Employee Information/ Educational Background/ Regular Education, the records with “Major Certificate” and “Graduation Date” within the report period will be used for report.

Note: the “Education Level” of the satisfied record will be the “New Qualification” and one of the record right before that record will be the “Old Qualification” in the report.

Form of Contract Commitment Change

In “Labor Fluctuation Input”, the records with “Fluctuation Date” within the report period will be used for report. The “Old” and “New” form of commitment will be get from these records.

Note:
If there are 2 or more records satisfying the filter condition, the priority is the record with the latest “Fluctuation Date” toward the “Date to”.

Employees with Changes (column 1 – 8)

The data of these columns are the same as ones in Labor - Ending

Employees with Changes (column 9)

The data of this column is the same as one in column 14 in Labor - Ending

Labor Contract Suspension

This part shows the employees who have fluctuation information (entered in “Labor Fluctuation Input): the “Suspension Date from” is before or the same as “Date to” and the “Suspension Date to” is after the “Date from” in filter condition.

Employees with Labor Contract Suspended (column 1 – 8)

The data of these columns are the same as ones in Labor - Ending

The Effective Date of the Labor Contract (column 9)

“Effective Date from” in “Labor Contract”; only contracts with latest “Date Signed” before or the same as “Date to” will be used for this report.

Be Employee Joining UI (column 10)

The data of this column is the same as one in column 14 in Labor - Ending

Labor Contract Suspension (column 11 & 12)

The “Contract Suspension Date” and “Contract Resuming Date” are the “Suspension Date from/to” defined in “Labor Fluctuation Input”.
