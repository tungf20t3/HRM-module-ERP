Payment Term List

This list is used to define payment methods (cash, transfer…) and payment days (number of days a customer is allowed for deferred payment). A payment term has a specific payment method and payment days.
Payment term is used in voucher entries relating to accounts receivable.
Payment term definition and entries support in reports relating to receivables aging such as Receivables Aging Report, Due Invoice Report …
Payment method will appear in printed invoice only.

See “Hints and Tips in List Setting” for details of common tasks in list maintenance
