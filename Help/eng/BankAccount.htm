Bank Account List

Path: Modules → Cash Management → Bank Account List
Bank account list is used to record data of your bank accounts. Bank account, bank name and city/province will be printed in Bank Debit Advice (Payment Order).

Field descriptions

Account

The account in “Chart of Account”; each account can be connected to only one bank account.

Bank Account

The account that your company registered in bank

Bank Name/ Other Name

Vietnamese and English name of bank; bank name is a required field.

City/Province

The city/province where the bank is located

Phone Number/ Fax Number/ Note

Phone number, fax number and note about the bank account

See “Hints and Tips in List Setting” for details of common tasks in list maintenance
