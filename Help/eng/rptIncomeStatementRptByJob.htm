Report form declaration - Type: 1 - Debit, 2 - Credit

This option is base to determine if the article will be get data from Debit Accounts or Credit Accounts defined in "Project/Job Article List".
- If an article is defined with Type 1 - Debit, it will be calculated by debit arising of accounts in list of Debit Accounts, corresponding to ones in Reference Debit Accounts, minus credit arising of these accounts corresponding to ones in list of Deduction Debit Accounts (These accounts are defined in Project/Job Article List).
- If an article is defined with Type 2 - Credit, it will be calculated by credit arising of accounts in list of Credit Accounts, corresponding to ones in Reference Credit Accounts, minus debit arising of these accounts corresponding to ones in list of Deduction Credit Accounts (These accounts are defined in Project/Job Article List).
