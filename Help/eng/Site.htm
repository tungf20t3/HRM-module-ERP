Warehouse/Site List

Path: Modules → Inventory Management → List Maintenance → Warehouse/Site List

Some notes in defining warehouse/site:

    · In case the optional parameter “Calculate common average cost of all sites by site group” is “Yes”, it is required to select site group in this menu. Site group is used in reporting and calculating average cost by site group.
    · The checkbox option “Agent” shows if the site is site of agency or company; supporting in stock report by site. In some reports, all sites of agencies (checked in “Agent” option) can be gathered in only 1 site in data view.

To items having consignment account, during data entry relating to these items, if the selected site is “Agent” site, inventory account will be auto replaced by consignment account.

See “Hints and Tips in List Setting” for details of common tasks in list maintenance
