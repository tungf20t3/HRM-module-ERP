Calculating Way

    If "0 – Base on Formula", the article will be calculated from the result of other articles
    If "1 – On Account Arising", the article will be calculated from accounting entry amount in period
    If "2 – On Opening Balance"¸ the article will be get from opening balance.

Receipt/Disbursement

    If "1 - Receipt", this article is cash increase article
    If "0 - Disbursement", this article is cash decrease article
