Article List

Data in reports are extracted from many books and reference, so the calculation articles should be defined in a list in order to make simple report forms and easy to check.
Path: Module → Investment Accounting → List Maintenance → Article List

Field descriptions

Debit Accounts/ Reference Debit Accts/ Deduction Debit Accts

“Debit accounts” is list of accounts from which the data in debit is extracted.
“Reference debit accounts” is list of accounts corresponding to debit accounts.
“Deduction debit accounts”: if there are deduction debit accounts, the article will be calculated by credit amounts of debit accounts minus debit amounts except ones corresponding to deduction debit accounts. Let this field blank if there is no deduction.

Credit Accounts/ Reference Credit Accts/ Deduction Credit Accts

“Credit accounts” is list of accounts from which the data in credit is extracted.
“Reference credit accounts” is list of accounts corresponding to credit accounts.
“Deduction credit accounts”: if there are deduction credit accounts, the article will be calculated by debit amounts of credit accounts minus credit amounts except ones corresponding to deduction credit accounts. Let this field blank if there is no deduction.

Balance Type

This option shows if the balance in debit or credit of the defined accounts will be used for this article. It is used in reports with balance such as Investment Capital, Detail Investment Capital…
