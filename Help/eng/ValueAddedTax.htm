Value Added Tax List

Path: Modules → General → Tax → Value Added Tax List

Fields descriptions

Tax Rate %

The rate of this tax type

VAT Account: Output

Output VAT account is used in case of issuing sales, service invoices. Then, output VAT account will be corresponding to Debit account in general information of invoice (such as cash, AR/AP account). However, it is editable in the field of account corresponding to tax account.

VAT Account: Output Deduction

Deduction output VAT account is used in cases of sales return and sales discount voucher entry. Deduction output VAT account will be corresponding to Credit account in general information of invoice (such as cash, AR/AP account). However, it is editable in the field of account corresponding to tax account.

VAT Account: Input

Input VAT account is used in case of goods, services purchase invoice entry. Input VAT account will be corresponding to Credit account in general information of invoice (such as cash, AR/AP account).

VAT Account: Input Deduction

Deduction input VAT account is used in cases of returns to supplier voucher entry.
Deduction input VAT account will be corresponding to Debit account in general information of invoice (such as cash, AR/AP account).

Note:
In voucher entry screens, output/ output deduction/ input/ input deduction VAT accounts are extracted from this list. However, they are editable.

Ordinal Number

Ordinal number of the tax code in tax reports.
