Approval screen

Some operations in PO Approval

Approve PO in “Open” status

Open “PO Approval” function, click on the “Select” check-box in required rows, then click " - Approve”. The approved PRs will disappear in this screen and changed to “Approved” status.

Cancel the Approval of “Approved” PO

Open “PO Approval” function, click on " - “Search” icon, select “2 - Approved” status in “Other” tab, click “Ok”. The approved POs will be listed. Tick on the “Select” check-box in required rows, then click “ - Cancel”. The selected PO will disappear in this screen and changed to “Open” status.

Cancel PR

Open “PO Approval” function, tick on the “Select” check-box in required rows, then click “ - Cancel”. The cancelled PO will disappear in this screen and changed to “Closed” status.
