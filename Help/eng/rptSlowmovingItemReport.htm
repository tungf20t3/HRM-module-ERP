Slow-moving Item Report

Filter condition

Slow-moving item report shows stock quantity (and value) of items with the latest issuing date before the "Date to" backward "Number of Days" in filter condition. To items received without issuing, the system will use the latest receiving date.

For example:
Item VTA received on 01 May 2013 with the quantity of 100 priced VND 100,000; on 15 May 2013, issuing 50 priced VND 100,000.
View report on 31 May 2013, number of days as 10, the item VTA will be shown in report with stock quantity of 50 and value of VND 5,000,000. The latest issuing date is 15 May 2013, before the report date 31 May 2013 backward 10 days so the item is considered as slow moving.
View report on 31 May 2013, number of days as 20, the item VTA will not be shown in report because the latest issuing date is 15 May 2013, after the report date 31 May 2013 backward 20 days.
View report on 10 May 2013, number of days as 5, the item VTA will be shown in report with stock quantity of 100 and value of VND 10,000,000. Because to 10 May 2013, this item has not been issued, the latest receiving date 01 May 2013 will be considered as the latest issuing date. It is before the report date 10 May 2013 backward 5 days so the item is considered as slow moving.
