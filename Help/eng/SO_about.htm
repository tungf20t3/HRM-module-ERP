Who should use the manual?

This manual is made for users to know how to run the system smoothly to finish tasks in process of Sales Management in AI ERP.

    - Accounting Dept.: in posting to GL;
    - Sales Dept.: in defining sale price, receiving and processing SO;
    - Inventory: in balance and planning for receipt.

Besides, program supplies management reports which support in strictly and exactly managing information of goods.

Main features of Sales Management

Sales Management helps automate all sale process of enterprise: from receiving Sale Orders to delivering and invoicing. You can operate all steps in process or reduce steps basing on specific nature of enterprise. Sales activities are more effective when operations and processes in delivering and receiving money are shortened. The fact that Sale Orders are quickly and in time processed will please customers and reduce SO changes and adjustments.
There are many other functions in this module: Sale Return, Alternative Goods and Debt Adjustment. Price and discount are also calculated in many ways in order to satisfy many different requirements of price management.

Special features of Sales Management module

Customer List

    - Unlimited customer records;
    - Keeps important customer contact information such as address, telephone, fax number, contact person, credit account, credit limit…;
    - Customer classification by different criteria: geography, company type and company size.

Discount

    - Flexible discount management allows declaring and applying many forms of discount. In AI ERP - Sales Management, discount can be calculated by customer, customer group, item, item group, site, or the combination of these factors to design more flexible discount forms;
    - Many discount forms in the same transaction (a customer can receive many discount forms in the same transaction).

Sales Price

    - Many sale prices or discounts according to order quantity;
    - Many forms of sale price by product, product type or customer;
    - Sale price in many kinds of foreign currency.

Sales Management

    - Controls receivables detailed by invoice, contract, period and settlement discount;
    - Flexible purchase process: Invoicing after issuing goods/ invoicing at once/ separately invoicing or creating Sales Invoice before creating Pick List;
    - Suspends orders of customers whose credit limits are over, who haven’t paid for pass due debt or have check returned because of lack of money in account;
    - Limits right of approval strictly to each functional department.

Import Sales Data from Excel File

This function is convenient for user to create data in any Excel file as template which are input into program and posted to GL then.

Report

    - Full sales report system detailed by customer, invoice, item…
    - Sales reports analyzed by time helps user evaluate transactions with customers;
    - Controls sales status as well as sales return from customers;
    - Catch achieved sales turnover by each customer, product…

Integration with other modules

Sales Management has integrated with Inventory Management, Accounts Receivable, Cash Management, and General Ledger.

Integration with other modules in AI ERP

Sales Management has integrated with other modules in AI ERP – some integration is required, some can be optional.

System

System has been related to Sales Management in Sales Department List; therefore, department list is used for both two modules.

General Ledger

General Ledger module plays the role of center receiving information of accounting entry from all other modules. In AI ERP 3.6, Sales Management module has integrated with General Ledger through:

    - Chart of Accounts
    In List Maintenance or Data Entry screens in Advanced Costing, there are some accounts declared in General Ledger such as Receivable Account, Item Account, Reason Account, Tax Account… Sales Management will be declared in accordance with account system organization in General Ledger to support system analysis.
    - Voucher Entry
    Some sales data (Sales Invoice, Receivable Vouchers, Sales Return…) saved in Sales Management will be kept processing or directly posted to ledger in General Ledger. It depends on status of vouchers when saved. General Ledger can intervene in original vouchers in Sales Management if it is given competence to adjust voucher.

Inventory Management

One of main objects managed by Sales Management is sites/ warehouses and goods, so there must be the integration between Sales Management and Inventory Management shown in:

    - Stock list system: Site information (sites, shelves, items, lots…) in Purchase Management is extracted from Inventory Management.
    - Vouchers posted to inventory books: Some sales data (Receipt, Sales Return…) in Sales Management will be posted to detailed books in Inventory Management after saved.

Accounts Receivable

Receivable information (sale on credit, payment terms…) in Accounts Receivable is extracted from Sales Management to create transactions. Data in Issuing Transaction of Sales Management will be posted to create sale invoices and many other steps in Accounts Receivable process.
The voucher entry process is designed different from businesses for specific requirements.
To select the right transaction code, clarify the following questions:

    - Is there pick list created before invoicing?
    - Is invoice created by Sales or Accounting department?

Supposing that the process in your business is creating pick list before invoicing, accountant will create invoice by getting data from pick list. The process will be:

    - Sales – Pick List;
    - Accountant – Invoice transaction code 3 – Invoice.

Supposing that the process in your business is immediate invoicing, the process will be:

    - Accountant – Invoice transaction code 1 – Receivable invoice.

Advanced Costing

Sales Management has integrated with Advanced Costing in planning material requirement for manufacture basing on quantity in every MO of SO.

Cash Management

Sales Management has integrated with Cash Management in payment detailed by invoices. In some paying processes, payments are not only defined and controlled details in Accounts Receivable but also defined and allocated when creating Receipt Transactions. In this case, Receipt or Bank Credit Advice will be filtered the invoices or receivable vouchers created in Sales Management.
