Accumulation Account List

Job accumulation account list helps to identify the jobs which are required accumulation tracking, accumulation balance entering, accumulation reporting by job and accumulation copying to next years.
Path: Modules → Job Management → List Maintenance → Accumulation Account List

Field descriptions

Account

Accumulation account is extracted from chart of accounts. Only when the selected account is accumulation account, it is possible to record job opening accumulation by this account and create accumulation reports.

Debit/Credit

This checkbox option, if activated, indicates that accumulation will be calculated on debit and vice versa.
