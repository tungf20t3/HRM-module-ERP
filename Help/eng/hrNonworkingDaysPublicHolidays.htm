Non-working Days, Public Holidays

This menu is used to define the non-working days and holidays for the system processes in workday and annual leave computation.

In annual leave management, if an employee takes annual leave many days in which there is a public holiday. The way this day is recorded depends on the selection in "Priority" (1 - By Defined Workday, 2 - By Non-working Day or Public Holiday). If the priority is 2 - By Non-working Day or Public Holiday, this day will be recorded as "Public Holiday" and the employee will not be subtracted a day of annual leave.

Entry screen

Field descriptions

Shift/Workday

The shift and workday of the non-working day/ public holiday will be recorded in timesheet.

Regular Hours

Number of hours of the non-working day will be recorded in timesheet.

Category

Selected from Leave Type List; only leave type with category 1 - Holiday will appear.
