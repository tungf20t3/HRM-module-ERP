Descriptions of data generating

- Article [21]:

Counts number of individuals getting income which belongs to “A01”, “A02” or “B01” Income Type, deducted PIT in the year and taxable income is greater than 0. If there are many records of income in these types for one individual, the system will count 1 only;

- Article [22]:

Counts number of individuals getting income which belongs to “A01” Income Type, deducted PIT in the year and taxable income is greater than 0. If there are many records of income in this type for one individual, the system will count 1 only;

- Article [23]:

Counts number of individuals getting income which belongs to “A01”, “A02”, “B01” Income Type and have deducted PIT in the year greater than 0;

- Article [24]:

Counts number of individuals getting income which belongs to “A01”, “A02” Income Type and have deducted PIT in the year greater than 0;

- Article [25]:

Counts number of individuals getting income which belongs to “B01” Income Type and have deducted PIT in the year greater than 0;

- Article [28]:

- Article [30]:

Total “Taxable Income” of arising with “Income Type” as B01 in year, added “Income” with the same “Income Type” input in “Deductible PIT Adjustment”;

Total “Taxable Income” of arising with “Income Type” as A01, A02, B01 and “Tax Amount” > 0, added “Income” with the same “Income Type” input in “Deductible PIT Adjustment”;

- Article [32]:

Total “Taxable Income” of arising with “Income Type” as A01, A02 and “Tax Amount” > 0, added “Income” with the same “Income Type” input in “Deductible PIT Adjustment”;

- Article [33]:

- Article [44]:
