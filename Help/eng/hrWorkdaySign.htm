Workday Sign List

This list is used to define workday signs which will be used during the workday computation progress.

- Non-working Days, Public Holidays;
- Detailed Shift;
- Employee Card Info Input;
- Employee Leave Input;
- Employee Time In/Out Adjusting;
- Employee Leave by Regulation.

Entry screen

Field Descriptions

Sign

Note:
The general questions before creating new workday sign:
- Is the workday category different?
- Is workday coefficient different?
- Are the other benefits of workday different?
If there is 1 "Yes" answer for these 3 questions, it is required to create a new workday sign.

Workday Type

This field is used to determine which workday sign is regular workday overtime, working on non-working days or public holidays which will be shown in "Overtime Sheet".
Workday type is extracted from workday type list; used to define regular or overtime workday in detailed shift ("Category": 1 - Regular, 2 - Overtime).

Salary Category

1 - By Time, 2 - Product, 3 - Piece Rate and 9 - Other

Category

There are 3 options: 1 - Regular, 2 - Night Work and 9 - Other. This category is used to define taxable income in Personal Income Tax module.

Coefficient

The coefficient for salary computation of each workday sign

For example:
Coefficient of the workday in Sunday (overtime) is defined as 2; it means that the salary for Sunday working day equals 200% of the regular workday.
