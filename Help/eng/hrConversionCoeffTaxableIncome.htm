Field descriptions

Conversion Income

The net income that will be converted to taxable income, based on the progressive tax tariff

Coefficient

The coefficient is base to convert net income to taxable income

For example:
Conversion income is: VND 9,250,000;
Taxable income will be: (9,250,000 - 250,000)/0.9 = 10,000,000 (VND)
