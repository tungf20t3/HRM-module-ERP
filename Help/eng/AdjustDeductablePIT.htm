Adjust Deductable PIT

This function use to adjust Taxable income and deducted PIT after finalization.

Adjusted Income

Effect to the articles in Declaration of PIT Finalization for Paying Salaries and Wages(05/KK-TNCN)”:

    - Input “Taxable Income” of arising with “Income Type” as A02 will effect to the articles 26 and 30
    - Input “Taxable Income” of arising with “Income Type” as B01 will effect to the articles 27 and 31

Adjusted PIT

Effect to the articles in Declaration of PIT Finalization for Paying Salaries and Wages(05/KK-TNCN)”:

    - Input PIT of arising with “Income Type” as A01 will effect to the article 37
    - Input PIT of arising with “Income Type” as A02 will effect to the article 38
    - Input PIT of arising with “Income Type” as B01 will effect to the article 35

Notes: This function applying data closing by unit;
