Descriptions of data generating

- Article [21]:

Total “Taxable Income” of Income from capital investments (with “Income Type” as C01)

- Article [22]:

Total “Deducted PIT” of Income from capital investments (with “Income Type” as C01)

- Article [23]:

Total “Taxable Income” Income from capital, security transfers (with “Income Type” as C02)

- Article [24]:

Total “Deducted PIT” of Income from capital, security transfers (with “Income Type” as C02)

- Article [25]:

income being royalties, from franchises (with “Income Type” as C04)

- Article [26]:

Total “Deducted PIT” of Income from income being royalties, from franchises (with “Income Type” as C04)

- Article [27]:

Income being winnings or prizes, from inheritances or receipt of a gift (with “Income Type” as C04)

- Article [28]:

Total “Deducted PIT” of Income being winnings or prizes, from inheritances or receipt of a gift (with “Income Type” as C04)

- Article [29]:

Counts number of non-resident individuals in the year that gets income which belongs to “B01,B02,B03,B04” Income Type.

- Article [30]:

Total “Taxable Income” of non-resident individuals

- Article [31]:

Deducted PIT of non-resident individuals
