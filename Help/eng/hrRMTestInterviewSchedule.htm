Test and Interview Scheduling

This function is used to input the examination rounds of each recruitment period. This information will be auto-updated to “Test and Interview Schedule by Applicant”.
It is required to determine the “Round Code” and the “Position Code” in this function. Besides, there are some optional fields:

    - Interviewer;
    - Date of Interview;
    - Time of Interview;
    - Place of Interview;
    - Content;
    - Note.

The test and interview schedule cannot be edited if there is an examination round with applicant being checked to select in “Test and interview schedule by applicant”.
