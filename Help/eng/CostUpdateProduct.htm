Update Cost for Finished Goods

When receiving finished products (daily), we do not know how many received products cost (cost of production). It is monthly carried out after calculating cost of product. “Update Cost for Finished Goods” is the final step in cost of production calculating process. Related accounting books will be systematically updated.

Important Note:
To update cost of finished products correctly, it is required to check in "Group when Posting" option to costing objects (Department / Product / MO...) in "Voucher Information" definition.
