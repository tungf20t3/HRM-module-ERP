Training Reports

Training Requirements

Filter condition

Some special fields

Date from/to

The training requirements are filtered by the “Date” in “Training Request Form”; only requirements with the “Date” within this “Date from/to” will be shown in this report.

Training Date from/to

Filtered by the Request Date from/to in “Training Request Form”

Training Plan

Filter condition

Some special fields

Training Date from/to

Filtered by “Schedule Date from” in “Training Course Information”

Status

Training courses with status satisfying the filter condition will be shown in report. There are 4 statuses: 1 – Planned, 2 – Opening, 3 – Completed and 4 – Cancelled.

“From Date”/ “To Date”

These columns are retrieved from “Schedule Date from/to” in Training Course Information/ General information.

“No. of Trainees”, “Total Cost”

These are planned number of trainees and total planned cost defined in Training Course Information/ Other information.

Training Implementation

Filter condition

Some special fields

Training Date from/to

Filtered by “Training Date from” in Training Course Information/ General information

Status

Training courses with status satisfying the filter condition will be shown in report. There are 4 statuses: 1 – Planned, 2 – Opening, 3 – Completed and 4 – Cancelled.

“From Date”/ “To Date”

These columns are retrieved from “Training Date from/to” in Training Course Information/ General information.

“No. of Trainees”, “Total Cost”

These are actual number of trainees and total actual cost defined in Training Course Information/ Other information.

Training Result Detailed by Course

Filter condition

Some special fields

Training Date from

Filtered by the “Training Date from” in Training Course Result

“Mark”

It will be shown in form of “Mark/ Maximum Mark”. In case there is no “Maximum Mark”, only “Mark” will be shown.

“Department”, “Position”

Retrieved from “Training Course Result” input

Note:
The data for this report are retrieved from “Training Course Result” only. (Not from Employee Information/ Training Courses)

Trainee Qualifying and Result

Filter condition

Some special fields

Training Date from

Filtered by the “Training Date from” in Training Course Result

“Mark”

It will be shown in form of “Mark/ Maximum Mark”. In case there is no “Maximum Mark”, only “Mark” will be shown.

“Department”, “Position”

Retrieved from current department and position of the employee

Note:
The data for this report are retrieved from Employee Information/ Educational Background/ Training Courses. The information is directly input (for the training courses before using the system) or systematically updated from the “Training Course Result” (when using AI ERP and forwards).

Training Requirement Report by Job Position

Filter condition

Some special fields

Report Date and Options

Option: 1 – Show Uncompleted Subjects only, 2 – Completed, * - All.
The system will select the employees in positions, departments and subjects defined in “Training Requirement Input by Job Position” with the “Effective Date” before or the same as the “Report Date”.
Besides, the “Uncompleted” or “Completed” option with the “Passed” or “Failed” result will be shown following the result in “Employee Information/ Educational Background/ Training Courses”.

“Department”, “Position”

Retrieved from working history of the employee (the latest department and position before report date)
