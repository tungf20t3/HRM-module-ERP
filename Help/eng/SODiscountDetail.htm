Discount Details

Path: Modules --> Sales Management --> Data Entry --> Discount Details
This function supports in defining detailed discount of each discount type defined in “Discount List”.

“Discount Detail List” by item form

Discount Detail List” by item form will be included these informations:

    Item
    UOM
    Quantity from
    Discount Amount
    Effective Date

“Discount Detail List” by customer form

“Discount Detail List” by customer form will be included these informations:

    Customer
    Discount Amount
    Effective Date

“Discount Detail List” by site + customer form

“Discount Detail List” by site + customer form will be included these informations:

    Site
    Customer
    Discount Amount
    Effective Date

“Discount Detail List” by site + item form

“Discount Detail List” by site + item form will be included these informations:

    Site
    Goods
    Uom
    Quantity from
    Discount Amount
    Foreign currency code
    Effective Date
