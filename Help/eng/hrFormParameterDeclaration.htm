Report Form Parameter Declaration

This function is used to define the codes of data which will be shown in columns of the report: detail labor fluctuation notification, number of labor fluctuation notification and book of labor.

Detail Labor Fluctuation Notification

Education information

Allow strings with many education codes: technical workers with vocational certificate, under-3-month vocational certificate, 3-month to 1-year vocational certificate, intermediate professional diploma, college degree, university degree or higher (separated by comma).

Contract Information

Allow strings with many types of contract: indefinite, from 12 months to under 36 months, from 3 months to under 12 months, under 3 months (separated by comma).

Reason for leaving information

Allow strings with many reasons: retired, unilateral termination, technology structure change, agreed termination, other reasons (separated by comma).

Number of Labor Fluctuation Notification

Education Information

Allow strings with many education codes: intermediate professional diploma, college degree, university degree or higher, primary vocational certificate, regular vocational training, untrained (separated by comma).

Contract Information

Allow strings with many types of contract: indefinite, definite, under-12-month (separated by comma).

Reason for leaving Information

Allow strings with many reasons: retired, unilateral termination, agreed termination, dismissal, other reasons (separated by comma).

Book of Labor

Timekeeping/ workday codes

Allow multi-code (separated by comma) with annual leave, other leave (paid), unpaid leave, sick off, maternity and overtime (150%, 200%, 300%) which will be get data and shown in Book of Labor in the corresponding columns: days of annual leave, days of other paid leave, days of unpaid leave, days of sick off leave, days of maternity leave, OT hours (150%), OT hours (200%), OT hours (300%).
