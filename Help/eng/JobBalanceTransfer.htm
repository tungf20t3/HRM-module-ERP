Copy Job Balance to Next Year

At the end of a fiscal year, it is required to copy closing balance to next year. For jobs, works, closing balance must also be copied by function “Copy Job Balance to Next Year”
Copy Job Balance to Next Year Method

Example:
To copy closing balance of year 2012 to year 2013, key in “Year from”: 2012, the system will copy to year 2013. Click “Ok”
