List of Dependants (05-3/BK-QTT-TNCN)

Report Layout

This report shows the deductions for dependants of resident individuals having income as salary or wages and labor contracts in a term of more than 3 months.

Report Field Descriptions

This report shows the employees (taxpayers) with “Income Type” as “A01” (extracted from year PIT file). Dependant information is extracted from “Input Employee Information”/ “Dependant Information” tab.
Dependants will be shown in the report when the records in “Dependant Information” having the effective date within the period of report (“Effective from” before or the same as last month of report year and “Effective to” after or the same as the first month of report year). In case the “Effective to” is blank, the record is always effective.
