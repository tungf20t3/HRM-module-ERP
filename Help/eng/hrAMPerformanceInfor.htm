Entry Screen

Some field descriptions

Format View – Upper Area

Employee

Employees existing in employee list and belonging to the assigned departments

Department/ Position/ Job Grade

These fields are systematically retrieved from “Working History” of employee by the “Date of Appraisal”.

Join Date

Retrieved from join date of the employee in “Employee Information”

Date of Appraisal

Default to be the date of entry but editable

Years of Service

Years of service is equal to “Date of Appraisal” minus “Join Date”

Years in Current Position

Years in current position will be equal to “Date of Appraisal” minus the “Start Date” of the latest official position (declared in Working History/ Current Working History) before “Date of Appraisal”. In case there is no information in “Working History”, the system will calculated by “Official Hired Date”.

Criteria and Opinion

In “Criteria” tab, click the icon to retrieve data from “Appraisal Criteria” function by employee selected in format view.

Self-appraisal/ Manager Appraisal

These fields are used to input the appraisal scores, except criteria level 1. The scores of criteria level 1 will be system-calculated following the formula:

Score of criteria level 1 = ∑ [(score of each direct-under criterion level 2 x Corresponding weight)/Total weight]

Other tabs are used to input opinions or comments of appraisers (direct supervisor and senior manager) and appraisee.

Format view – Lower area

Total score

Total score is calculated by the formula:

Total score = ∑ [(Score of each criterion level 1 x Corresponding weight)/Total weight]
