PIT Assessable Income Type List

Entry screen

Defining assessable income type used in income list declaration. One assessable income type may include some income codes. This list is fixed, you can only edit fields different from “Income Type” field, including:

    A01 - Income being salary (resident taxpayers with labor contracts in term of 3 months or over);
    A02 - Income being salary (resident taxpayers without labor contracts or with ones in term of under 3 months);
    B01 - Income being salary (non-resident taxpayers);
    B02 - Business income with 1% tax rate (non-resident taxpayers);
    B03 - Business income with 2% tax rate (non-resident taxpayers);
    B04 - Business income with 5% tax rate (non-resident taxpayers);
    C01 - Income from capital investments;
    C02 - Income from capital, security transfers;
    C03 - Income from real property transfers;
    C04 - Income being royalties, from franchises;
    C05 - Income being winnings or prizes, from inheritances or receipt of a gift;
    C06 - Income from capital transfer of non-resident individual;
    D01 - Incomes from indemnities under the oversea contract for insurance.

Field descriptions

PIT Type

If this type is 1 – Progressive, assessable income will be calculated according to the scale of the progressive tax tariff. If this type is 2 – Entire, each type of income will be applied a specific tax rate.
See “Income/Deduction List” for more details.

Replacement Code

Code used to replace income type according to the filter condition when running PIT finalization (1-Yes) If the “Replace Income Type” option is 1 – Yes, the value of “Income Type” column arising will be replaced by this code. If there is no replacement code, income type will be the old one.
In case an employee has received income being salary deducted PIT by both the progressive tax and the flat rate tariff in a year:

For example:
2 probation months with income (A02) deducted by flat rate tariff, 10 official working months with income (A01) deducted by the progressive tax tariff. The year-end finalization is calculated by the progressive tax tariff; only income code A01 will be used so income code A02 must be replaced by A01.
