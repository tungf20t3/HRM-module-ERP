Transfer Balance to Next Year

Path: Path: Modules → General Ledger → Transfer Balance to Next Year


This function is used to transfer account balance and customer/supplier balance to next fiscal year.
Other balances as inventory, job, fixed asset value… are processed in specific modules.
The balance can be transferred to next fiscal year without data closing; therefore, it can be re-transferred if there is any data adjustment.
Open “Transfer balance to next year” function, key in the year of which the balance will be transferred, the destination year (next year) will auto appear.
Click “OK” for the transfer processed or “Cancel” to cancel the transfer.

Note:
The system will process the balance transfer by calendar year; therefore, if the fiscal year is different from the calendar year, the balance must also be transferred at the end of December.
