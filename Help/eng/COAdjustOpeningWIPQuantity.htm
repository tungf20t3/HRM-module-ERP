Adjust Opening Quantity of WIP

Entry Screen

Field Descriptions

Factor Type

Type of factor WIP declaration is used in case finish rates of cost factor types closing are not equal.

Product

Code of product WIP opening adjusted

Department

Code of production line, workshop in case product WIP by line, workshop. This is a required field if cost is calculated by department in system parameter.

MO Number

Number of MO WIP in case product WIP in MO. This is a required field if cost is calculated by MO in system parameter.

WIP Quantity

Quantity of product WIP opening

Finish Rate

Finish rate of product WIP opening. If WIP rate is 50%, finish rate will be entered 50.

Exchange Quantity

Quantity of finished products exchanged from products WIP closing
