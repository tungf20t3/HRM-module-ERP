Recruitment Reports

Recruitment Requirements

The data for this report are retrieved from “Recruitment Requisition Form”.

Recruiting Date from/to

Filtered by “Request Date from/to” in the “Recruitment Requisition Form”

Request Date from/to

Filtered by “Request Date” in the “Recruitment Requisition Form”

    - The other fields are also filtered by the corresponding information in “Recruitment Requisition Form";
    - The system will check the “view” right of the current user;
    - The “view” right will be checked by unit. Only requisitions of the departments in the assigned unit can be viewed.

Recruitment Information

The data for this report are retrieved from “Recruitment Information Input”; following the “Date from” in filter condition.

Date from/to

Filtered by the “Date from/to” field in the “Recruitment Period Information Input"

    - The other fields are also filtered by the corresponding information in “Recruitment Period Information Input";
    - The system will check the “view” right of the current user;
    - The “view” right will be checked by unit. Only requisitions of the departments in the assigned unit can be viewed;
    - The access right to report will be checked by department.

Applicant Profiles

The data for this report are retrieved from “Applicant Profile Input”; only applicants with the “Date Applied” within the period of “Date Applied from/to” in filter condition are shown.

Date Applied from/to

Filtered by the “Date Applied” in “Applicant Profile Input”

    - The other fields are also filtered by the corresponding information in “Applicant Profile Input";
    - The system will check the “view” right of the current user;
    - The “view” right will be checked by unit. Only applicants in the recruitment period of the departments in the assigned unit can be viewed.

Test and Interview Schedules

The data for this report are retrieved from “Test and Interview Scheduling”; only applicants in recruitment round and recruitment period with the “Date from/to” satisfying the filter condition will be shown.

Date from/to

Filtered by the “Date from/to” defined in “Test and Interview Scheduling"

    - The other fields are also filtered by the corresponding information in “Test and Interview Scheduling";
    - The system will check the “view” right of the current user;
    - The “view” right will be checked by unit. Only recruitment periods of the departments in the assigned unit can be viewed;
    - The access right to report will be checked by department.

Test and Interview Result

The data for this report are retrieved from “Test Result Input” and “Interview Result Input”. Only applicants, positions and recruitment periods with the “Date of Test” satisfying the filter condition will be shown.

Date from/to

Filtered by the “Date from/to” defined in “Test Result Input" (or “Interview Result Input”) with the “Date of Test” satisfying the filter condition

Test Type

Filtered by the test type in grid of “Test Result Input”

Status

Filtered by “Applicant Form Status” in general information of “Test Result Input”

    - The other fields are also filtered by the corresponding information in “Test Result Input";
    - The system will check the “view” right of the current user;
    - The “view” right will be checked by unit. Only recruitment periods of the departments in the assigned unit can be viewed;
    - The access right to report will be checked by department.

Recruitment Activity Summary Report

The data for this report are retrieved from “Recruitment Period Information Input”, “Applicant Profile Input”, “Test Result Input” and “Interview Result Input”. The system will count the applicants in position and recruitment period defined in “Recruitment Period Information Input” with the date satisfying the filter condition.

Filter condition

Date from/to

Filtered by the “Date” defined in “Recruitment Period Information Input" satisfying the filter condition

    - The other fields are also filtered by the corresponding information input;
    - The system will check the “view” right of the current user;
    - The “view” right will be checked by unit. Only recruitment periods of the departments in the assigned unit can be viewed;
    - The access right to report will be checked by department.

Some report fields

“Required” quantity

This quantity is counted from recruitment periods information input.

“Applicant” quantity

The system will count number of applicants with the status 2 – Accepted in “Applicant Profile Input”.

“Accepted” quantity

The system will count number of applicants with the status 1 – Hired after the “Recruitment Decisions” are input.

“Recruitment Period”/ “Passed” quantity/ “Failed” quantity

These quantities are counted from Test and Interview Result Input.

Note: Each applicant is counted once even though there are many records.

Vacancy Status Report

The system will use the number of employees in “Employee Requirement Input” to compare will the current number of employees in “Employee Information”.

Filter condition

Filter by

    1 – Insufficient Diff.: retrieve the records with “Insufficient Diff.” greater than 0;
    2 – Excessive Diff.: retrieve the records with “Excessive Diff.” greater than 0;
    3 – Both Insufficient and Excessive Diff.: retrieve the records with “Insufficient Diff.” or “Excessive Diff.” greater than 0;
    * - All: retrieve all records

Some report fields

Required Q’ty

Retrieved from “Employee Requirement Input"

Current Q’ty

The current number of employees in “Employee Information”

Excessive Difference

Absolute number of current quantity minus required quantity if it is greater than 0

Insufficient Difference

Absolute number of current quantity minus required quantity if it is smaller than 0

Recruitment Statistic by Channel/Source

The data for this report are retrieved from “Applicant Profile Input”, “Test/ Interview Result Input/ Application Form Status” and “Recruitment Decision”.

Filter condition

“Applicant” quantity

Counted from “Applicant Profile Input”

“Considering/ Satisfied/ Denied” quantity

Counted from “Applicant Profile Input” and “Test/ Interview Result Input”

“Accepted” quantity

Counted by the applicants with “1 – Hired” status after inputting ‘Recruitment Decision”
