Template Information...

This function is used to define the common information for mass of vouchers and reports according to Decision, Circular… of the Ministry of Finance.

The template information

Information is define in the order of rows (usually 4 rows).

For example Cash Receipt according to Circular No. 200:
1.Name: “Mẫu số 01-TT”
Other Name: Form 01-TT
2.Name: (Ban hành theo QĐ số
Other Name: (Issued with Decision No.
3.Name: 200/2014/TT-BTC ngày 22/12/2014
Other Name: 200/2014/TT-BTC Dated Dec 22th 2014
4.Name: của Bộ trưởng BTC)
Other Name: of the Ministry of Finance)

Some tasks

1-New

Used in case you want to create new templates on the basic of the definition of the available ones

2-Edit

Enable to edit the created templates before (1 – New)

3-Delete

Enable to delete the created of edited templates; check on “Confirm Delete” box and click “Ok” to delete the template

9-Apply

This type is selected when you want to print vouchers or reports in some template.
