Section List

Section is used as a cost aggregating center. These cost aggregating centers are also defined in “Department List” in System module. This menu is only used to define if the department is direct or indirect.
Path: Modules → Advanced Costing → List Maintenance → Department List

Field descriptions

Section

Department (section) of which cost is aggregated for product costing; extracted from “Department List”

Direct

This checkbox option shows that the department is intermediate section (unmark) or direct cost aggregation one (mark).
In cost factor list, if a factor is selected to be “Gathered by Dept.”, the arising cost must be aggregated by direct section. If a factor is selected to be “Gathered by Indirect Dept.”, the arising cost is aggregated by indirect section.

Prev. Section

Previous section is used in case there are many levels of section to know the level of the current section.
This manual has not mentioned previous section.
