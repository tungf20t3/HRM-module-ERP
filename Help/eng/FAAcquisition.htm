Fixed Asset Acquisition

Path: Modules → Fixed Assets Management → List Maintenance → Fixed Asset Acquisition Reason List
Fixed asset acquisition reason list is used to define the reason that asset is increased or decreased. They are also used in fixed asset information entries and decrease/increase asset reports.

Field descriptions

Type

The acquisition type is used to determine which reason is increase and which reason is decrease.
