Declare fiscal year

This function is used to declare the start date of fiscal year according to the applicable accounting system in each company.

Usually, the start date of the fiscal year is the first of January (according to VAS). However, for some FDI companies, the start date of the fiscal year may be different, then the analyzing periods in financial statements will be counted from the start date.
Some periodic statements in this case are:

    - Trial Balance;
    - Balance Sheet;
    - Income Statement;
    - Direct Cash Flow Statement;
    - Tool and Supply Book;
    - Tool and Supply Allocation Summary Report;
    - Job Accumulation Summary;
    - Income Statement by Job
    - Investment Capital;
    - Detail of Investment Capital;
    - Investment Implementation by Project, Work, Work Detail;
    - Investment Liquidation by Project, Work, Work Detail;
    - Investment Implementation;
    - …

For example:
The start date of the fiscal year is 01 April, the beginning input date is 01 Jan 2010. Account 111 has:
- Debit 500 on 15 Jan 2010
- Debit 1000 on 01 Mar 2010
- Debit 1500 on 01 Apr 2010
- Debit 2000 on 01 Sep 2010
View the Trial Balance from 01 Sep to 30 Sep 2010, “Debit accumulation from year-opening to period-opening” will be 1500.
If the start date of fiscal year is 01 Jan, the result will be 3000.
