Account Opening Balance Adjustment

This function is used to adjust the opening balance of accounts of previous year because of the mistakes, without adjusting the whole accounting data of the previous year.
You can enter (FC) debit balance or (FC) credit balance of account, the system will use this balance as the adjustment of opening balance of the previous year.

After the adjustment is entered, all data in the balance sheet will be completely changed and cannot be restored even though the adjustment is deleted. If you don’t want to use the adjustment any more, use the “Transfer Balance to Next Year” function.
