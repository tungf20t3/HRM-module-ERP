List of Accounts Mutually Balanced among Units

This definition shows if general accounts are balanced among units or sum of each unit

For example:
- Unit A has day-opening balance on 01/01/2011 of account 3334 as VND15.000.000.
- Unit B has day-opening balance on 01/01/2011 of account 3331 as VND16.000.000; debit balance of account 3334 as VND6.000.000
- On 15/01/2011, unit A has debit arising of account 3331 as VND4.000.000; debit arising of account 3334 as VND5.000.000.
- On 15/01/2011, unit B has credit arising of account 3331 as VND2.000.000; credit arising of account 3334 as 3.000.000
If account 333 is not declared in List of Accounts Mutually Balanced among Units:
- Opening debit balance of account 3331 is 0 and credit one is VND16.000.000.
- Opening debit balance of account 3334 is VND21.000.000 and credit one is 0.
- Opening debit balance of account 333 is VND15.000.000 and credit one is VND10.000.000
If account 333 is declared in List of Accounts Mutually Balanced among Units:
- Opening debit balance of account 333 is VND5.000.000=(15.000.000 + 6.000.000 - 16.000.000) and credit one is 0.

List of AR-AP Accounts with Balance in One Site

List of AR-AP accounts are not required to show balance in 2 sites (debit and credit) but balanced between debit and credit if there is balance in 2 sites of 2 different customers. For example: Account 141

List of AR-AP Accounts with Balance in Two Sites

List of common accounts (not AR-AP accounts) are not balanced between debit and credit but shown balance in both 2 sites.
