Changing Labor's Information Input

This function is used to enter the information changes of employees who joins social and health insurances. These information will be shown in insurance report form TK2-TS.

Some field descriptions

Employee ID

This is a required field, extracted from employee information. Only employees in the assigned unit will be shown to select.

SI Contribution Amount/ Payment Method/ Other Content/ Including Document

The information entered in these fields can be shown in article 11, 11.1, 12 and IV of the declaration form TK2-TS.

Effective Date

Employee ID and effective date must not be duplicated. The system will get records with latest effective date before the report date to the report.
