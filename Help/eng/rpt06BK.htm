Personal Income Tax on Transfering Values of Securities Listing (06/BK-TNCN)

Report Layout

Descriptions of data generating

- “Total Value of Transfers in Period”:

Total “Taxable Income” of arising with “Income Type” as A01 and the value of Adjusted Taxable Income with “Income Type” as C02.

- “Amount of PIT Deducted”:

“Tax Amount” of arising with “Income Type” as A01 and the value of Adjusted Taxable Income with “Income Type” as C02.
