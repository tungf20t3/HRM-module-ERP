PIT Declaration for Paying Salaries and Wages (05/KK–TNCN)

Report Layout

Descriptions of data generating

Article [21]:

Counts number of individuals getting income which belongs to “A01”, “A02” or “B01” Income Type, deducted PIT in the period and taxable income is greater than 0. If there are many records of income in these types for one individual, the system will count 1 only;

Article [22]:

Counts number of individuals getting income which belongs to “A01” Income Type, deducted PIT in the period and taxable income is greater than 0. If there are many records of income in this type for one individual, the system will count 1 only;

Article [23]:

Counts number of individuals getting income which belongs to “A01”, “A02”, “B01” Income Type and have deducted PIT in the period greater than 0;

Article [24]:

Counts number of individuals getting income which belongs to “A01”, “A02” Income Type and have deducted PIT in the period greater than 0;

Article [25]:

Counts number of individuals getting income which belongs to “B01” Income Type and have deducted PIT in the period greater than 0;

Article [26]:

Total “Taxable Income” of income with “Income Type” as A01, A02 and B01;

Article [27]:

Total “Taxable Income” of income with “Income Type” as A01 and A02;

Article [28]:

Total “Taxable Income” of income with “Income Type” as B01;

Article [29]:

Total “Taxable Income” of income with “Income Type” as A01, A02, B01and “Tax Amount” > 0;

Article [30]:

Total “Taxable Income” of income with “Income Type” as A01, A02 and “Tax Amount” > 0;

Article [31]:

Total “Taxable Income” of income with “Income Type” as B01 and “Tax Amount” > 0;

Article [32]:

Total PIT of income with “Income Type” as A01, A02, B01 deducted in period;

Article [33]:

Total PIT of income with “Income Type” as A01, A02 deducted in period;

Article [34]:

Total PIT of income with “Income Type” as B01 deducted in period;

Article [35]:

Total “Taxable Income” of income with “Income Type” as D01;

Article [36]: Total PIT of income with “Income Type” as D01 deducted in period.

Note:
To view this declaration, it’s required to run PIT calculation process first.
