Labor Fluctuation Input

This function is used to enter the data for “Detail of Labor Fluctuation Notification".

Field descriptions

Fluctuation Date

This is a required field and default as the system date.

Employee

This is a required field; extracted from employee list. Only employees in the department which you are assigned the “New” right will be shown to select.

Note:
If there is a record with the same Fluctuation Date and Employee, the system will give message and disable to save.

UI Joining Time

The unemployment insurance joining time will be shown in “Detail of Labor Fluctuation Notification”/ Part III. List of labor increased/ Column 10 – Total UI Joining Time (if any).

Old/ New Commitment

These information will be shown in “Detail of Labor Fluctuation Notification”/ Part IV. Changing Labor’s Information/ Column 16,17 – Old/New form of contract commitment.

Susp. Date from/to

These information will be shown in “Detail of Labor Fluctuation Notification”/ Part V. Labor contract suspension/ Column 11, 12 – Labor contract suspension/ resuming date.
