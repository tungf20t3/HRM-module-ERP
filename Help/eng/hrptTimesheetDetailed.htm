Detailed Time Record Sheet

Filter Condition

Descriptions

This kind of timesheet is used to show the detailed time record by workday of each employee by department and workday.

Actual Time In/Out

Actual time in/out of employees get from timekeeping data

Regular Time In/Out

Time in/out of employees after workday computation

In case employees work in a shift consists of 2 detailed shift but they check in/out at the first/last time when come in/out, the system will generate the time out of the first detailed shift and time in of the second one (following the time in and out defined in detailed shift).

For example:
- Shift 01: Office shift is split into 2 detailed shifts:
S1-Morning: Time in 08:00, time out 12:00;
C1-Afternoon: Time in 13:00, time out 17:00.
- Timekeeping data consists only time in of morning shift (08:00) and time out of afternoon shift (17:00).
- The report will be shown with 2 rows:
S1-Morning: Time in 08:00, time out 12:00;
C1-Afternoon: Time in 13:00, time out 17:00.
