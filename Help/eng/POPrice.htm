Supplier Price List

Entry screen

Field descriptions

Supplier ID

Extracted from “Supplier/Customer List”, required field.

Item Code/Supplier Item Code

Code of items, goods and supplier need to be updated price.

UOM/FC Code

Unit of measurement of items, goods and code of foreign currency in Quotation

Quantity from - to/ Min Order Qty/ Max Order Qty

Limit quantity of items applying price in Quotation. If this limitation is not declared, price in quotation will be applied to all quantity of items purchased. On the contrary, quantity of items purchased in this price can not be over limit.

Price

Unit price of items in Quotation

Lead Time

Purchase Lead Time (from order to receipt)

Quotation No./Quotation Date

Number and date of Quotation of supplier

Effective from/ Effective to

Date in which quotation has been effective (from beginning to ending). In order to choose supplier, system date must be within this period.
