Aggregate Arising Cost

Aggregate Arising Cost: aggregate arising cost in ledger by each factor and object declared in Cost Factor List; if aggregated objects are materials, aggregation includes quantity; to alternative materials, they are entered by “Group of Alternative Materials”.
Arising Cost can be aggregated as following table:

There are 2 kinds of cost in table: direct and indirect aggregated cost; cost calculated by quantity (item) and cost calculated only by value. There is no data in objects “Product Code”, “Dept.”, “MO” because of cost factor format.
To test the result of this calculation, enter report “Expenses Aggregation Report”
