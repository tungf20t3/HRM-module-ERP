Fixed Asset Disposal

The function is used to declare the disposal of asset in case of liquidation, sales, loss...
Path: Modules → Fixed Assets Management → Data Entry → Fixed Asset Disposal


Note:
If the “Fixed asset depreciated by day” option in “Optional Parameter” is “No”, the fixed asset will be still depreciated in the month of disposal. If you do not want the asset to be depreciated in this month, define the disposal date as the end date of previous month. On the contrary, the asset will be depreciated by using days (to disposal date).


Example:
1. An asset with value as 12,000,000; depreciated monthly by straight-line method in 12 months, beginning on 01/01/2010. On 14/02/2010, this asset is disposed. The depreciation value in February is 1,000,000 and in March is 0.
2. An asset with value as 12,000,000; depreciated daily by straight-line method in 12 months, beginning on 01/01/2010. On 15/02/2010, this asset is disposed. The depreciation value in February is 500,000 and in March is 0.
