The Scale of Progressive Tax Tariff

Entry screen

Used to calculate payable tax amount according to the scale of the progressive tax tariff: Tax Bracket Portion of Monthly Assessable Income (million dong) Tax Rate (%)

In the program, you can declare the tariff as following table:

Field descriptions

Tax Rate (%)

The tax rate in accordance with each assessable income; the current defined tax rates are from 5% to 35%.

Assessable Income over (Monthly)

The floor of assessable income in accordance with each tax bracket. For example: the floor of assessable income with tax rate 5% is 0 (it means that the assessable income is over 0 dong and under 5,000,001 dong). The same to the tax bracket 2 – tax rate 10%, the floor of assessable income is 5,000,000 dong (it means that the assessable income is over 5,000,000 dong and under the floor of next tax bracket).

Assessable Income over (Annual)

The floor of assessable income in year. Assessable Income over (Annual)=Assessable Income over (Monthly) *12
