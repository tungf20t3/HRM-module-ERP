Job Article List

Job article list is used to define the articles and in “Income Statement by Job”.
Path: Modules → Job Management → List Maintenance → Job Norm List

Field descriptions

Debit Accounts

List of accounts from which the data in debit is extracted

Reference Debit Accts

List of accounts corresponding to debit accounts

Deduction Debit Accts

If there are deduction debit accounts, the article will be calculated by credit amounts of debit accounts minus debit amounts except ones corresponding to deduction debit accounts. Let this field blank if there is no deduction.

Credit Accounts

List of accounts from which the data in credit is extracted

Reference Credit Accts

List of accounts corresponding to credit accounts

Deduction Credit Accts

If there are deduction credit accounts, the article will be calculated by debit amounts of credit accounts minus credit amounts except ones corresponding to deduction credit accounts. Let this field blank if there is no deduction.
