Fixed Asset Transfer

An asset, at first, is always defined to be used in a department. During the useful life, it can be transferred to another department. At that time, the “Fixed Asset Transfer” function is used define the destination department so that the depreciation reports by department can reflect the actual use of asset.
Path: Modules → Fixed Assets Management → Data Entry → Fixed Asset Transfer


The depreciation cost from the period when the asset is transferred will be allocated to depreciation account, expense account and destination department.
