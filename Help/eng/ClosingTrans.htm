Closing Entry Definition

Path: Modules → General Ledger → Definition → Closing Entry Definition
At the end of period, we usually make following closing entries:

    - Closing cost price account to business result account.
    - Closing sales turnover account to business result account.
    - Closing sales expense account to business result account.
    - Closing administration expense account to business result account.
    - …etc.

In case of management detailed in expense and revenue by business type and department, the quantity of closing entries may be very huge. Besides, when you need to have data adjusted, it is required to edit closing entries. So it is waste time if your entries are manually created.
Because the closing entries above are the same in accounting periods and the data extracting is clear, AI ERP supports auto closing entries.
For auto closing entries, we have to define “Source” and “Target” account for each entry, define closing from credit account to debit account and vice versa.
In case closing entries are detailed to customer, job, manufacturing order…, it is required to declare closing by customer, job, MO.
When processing closing entry, the arising amount in period will be closed and transferred to next period.
We have some cases in definition:

    - Closing from a sub-account to another one.
    - Closing from a general account to a sub-account. (Closing from many “Source” sub-accounts to a “Target” sub-account)
    - Closing from a general account to another one. (Closing by couple of “Source & Target” sub-accounts having the same level and “tail” – characters in sub-account belong to parent account)

Closing Entry List

Entry screen

Field descriptions

Ordinal Number

Ordinal number of closing entries by which the closing entries will be processed.

Note:
Closing entry numbers should be set apart from each other some characters in order to insert arising entries during closing process if necessary.

Description

Description of closing entry. This is required field and will be used as description in ledger of accounts defined in the closing entry.

Closing Type

Closing type is used to define “Source” and “Target” accounts in closing entry. Type 1 – closing debit account to credit one (642 - 911); type 2 – closing credit account to debit one (511 - 911) and type 3 – profit (loss) closing – the system will generate only profit or loss closing entry (default debit account is 911 and credit one is 4212).

Debit Account/ Credit Account

Debit (credit) account in closing entry; extracted from “Chart of Account”.

Closing Detail Entry only

If this option is active, only arising amounts detailed to customer, job, MO… are closed and transferred.

Closing by Customer/Job/MO

Check to close and transfer by customer/job/MO… in case closing entries are detailed to customer, job, MO...
