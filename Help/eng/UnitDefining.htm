Unit Information Definition

This function helps to declare unit information which will appear in vouchers, reports printing by unit. Details:
- Tax code, telephone number, bank account: appear in invoice print forms;
- Name of director, chief accountant and cashier: appear in vouchers and reports with these fields such as General Journal, General Ledger, Cash Receipt/Disbursement, Bank Credit/Debit Advice...

If there is no information defined in this "Unit Information Definition" function, the system will get information defined in optional parameters.
If unit information record existed with bank value, the program will also get information in here (not in optional parameters).
