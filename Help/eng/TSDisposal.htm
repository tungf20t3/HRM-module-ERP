Tool & Supply Disposal

The function is used to declare the disposal of tool & supply in case of liquidation, sales, loss...
Path: Modules → Tools & Supplies Management → Data Entry → Tool & Supply Disposal

In case of tool & supply allocation by time, if you do not want the asset to be depreciated in this month, define the disposal date as the end date of previous month.
The allocation of a disposed tool/supply will not appear in the allocation report in the months after disposal month.
