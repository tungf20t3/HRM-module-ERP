List of PIT on Salaries or Wages by Flat Rate Tariff (05-2/BK-QTT-TNCN)

Report Layout

Descriptions of data generating

- Column [11]:

Total “Assessable Income” after being adjusted of resident individuals with labor contracts in a term of less than 3 months, of non-resident individuals and from oversea insurance premiums;

- Column [12]:

Taxable income of premiums for life insurance and other optional insurance services provided by insurance companies not established in Vietnam;

- Column [13]:

Tax reducible income for individuals with labor contracts in a term of less than 3 months and non-resident individuals (working in the Economic Zone);

- Column [14]:

Tax reducible income for individuals with labor contracts in a term of less than 3 months and non-resident individuals (by agreements);

- Column [15]:

Deducted PIT of resident individuals with labor contracts in a term of less than 3 months, of non-resident individuals and from oversea insurance premiums;

- Column [16]:

Deducted PIT of premiums for life insurance and other optional insurance services provided by insurance companies not established in Vietnam

- Column [17]:

Reduced PIT for individuals with labor contracts in a term of less than 3 months and non-resident individuals (working in the Economic Zone).

Note:
To view this declaration, it’s required to run PIT calculation and PIT finalization process.
